What is basketball?

Basketball is a team sport in which two sets of five players compete to score points by shooting a ball through an elevated hoop. It was invented in 1891 by James Naismith and has since evolved into one of the world's most popular sports.

Basic Rules

  • The objective is to score points by putting the ball into the opponent's hoop.
  • Each team has five players on the field.
  • A game consists of four quarters of 10 or 12 minutes each, depending on the league.
  • It is allowed to bounce the ball, but not to walk with it without bouncing (Travel).

Famous leagues

  • NBA (National Basketball Association): The most important professional basketball league in the world.
  • EuroLeague: The top European basketball club competition.
  • WNBA: The professional women's basketball league in the United States.

Benefits of Basketball

Basketball is a sport that not only improves physical fitness, but also fosters skills such as teamwork, discipline and coordination.
